# Trader Dashboard Guide (Simple Version)

Open the dashboard here:
`http://127.0.0.1:8090/`

Think of this dashboard like a game control room for your trading bot.

## What Each Box Means

- `Summary`
  - Your scoreboard: total trades, win rate, and average PnL.

- `Open Trades`
  - How many trades are still active right now.

- `System Health`
  - Quick health check of the whole bot.
  - Green = good, Yellow = warning, Red = problem.

- `Risk Controls`
  - Safety rules (how strict trading is).
  - You can enable/disable auto test trading and set test size.

- `Top Candidates`
  - Best trade ideas found by the bot.

- `Signal Routes`
  - Shows what happened to each idea: approved or blocked, plus reason.

- `Execution Orders`
  - Real orders sent to broker/exchange.

- `Trade Intents`
  - Planned/attempted orders, including test/live intent rows.

- `Execution Learning`
  - What execution mistakes/failures happened and why.

- `Source Learning`
  - Which info sources are helping or hurting performance.

- `Learning Health (7d)`
  - How well the bot is learning from outcomes over the last 7 days.

- `Quant Validations`
  - Math checks before trades (EV, sample size, win rate, risk checks).

- `Event Alerts`
  - Big world/macro events that could affect trades.

- `Pipeline Signals (A/B/C)`
  - Signals generated by each strategy pipeline.

- `Copy-Trade Watch`
  - Tracked accounts/signals you follow.

- `Bookmarks (Trader)`
  - Saved links/research references.

## What The Buttons Do

- `Run Scan Now`
  - Runs the full scan and creates new candidates/routes.

- `Sync Broker`
  - Pulls latest order updates from broker.

- `Refresh Learning`
  - Rebuilds learning stats from latest outcomes.

- `Enable Auto Test`
  - Turns on automatic paper/test trading.

- `Disable Auto`
  - Stops auto trading.

- `Save HL $`
  - Sets Hyperliquid test order size.

## How It Works Together (Big Picture)

1. Pipelines find ideas (`Top Candidates`, `Pipeline Signals`, `Event Alerts`).
2. Safety + math checks filter them (`Risk Controls`, `Quant Validations`).
3. Passed ideas become routes (`Signal Routes`).
4. Approved routes place orders (`Execution Orders`, `Trade Intents`).
5. Broker sends updates back (`Sync Broker`).
6. Bot learns from wins/losses and failures (`Execution Learning`, `Source Learning`, `Learning Health`).
7. Better learning improves future decisions.

## Daily 1-Minute Routine

1. Check `System Health` first.
2. Keep `Enable Auto Test` on while testing.
3. Click `Run Scan Now`.
4. Review `Signal Routes` and `Execution Orders`.
5. Click `Sync Broker`, then `Refresh Learning`.
6. Check `Quant Validations` + `Learning Health` to see if quality is improving.

## Safety Rule

If health turns red or you see lots of blocked/failed rows, click `Disable Auto` and review the reasons before continuing.

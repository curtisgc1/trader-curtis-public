Kelly Signal — Position Sizing

Edge × Convexity × Risk Management. Kelly fraction = (p×b − (1−p)) / b. Only trade when Kelly > 0. Quarter Kelly applied by default.

Kelly Guide

pWin probability from historical outcomes
bPayout ratio: avg win% / avg loss%
Kelly%(p×b − (1−p)) / b — optimal capital fraction
¼ KellyFractional Kelly applied (25%) for humility
Convexityb ≥ 5 excellent · ≥ 2 good · < 1 concave (avoid)
BudgetSum of ¼Kelly across all positions ≤ 20% portfolio
skipNegative EV — don't bet
warmupThin data — treat as informational only
